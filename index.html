About This Page

Hi, my name is Ranski. I just want to tell a little story about love, a story that once made me believe happiness could stay forever. Everything felt normal until you came into my life and quietly changed everything. You became my safest place, my favorite person, and the reason my days suddenly felt lighter. With you, even silence felt comforting. And honestly, if someone asked me what love truly felt like, I would mention your name without hesitation. Because for me, you were never just a person in my story. You were the home my heart had been searching for all this time. Kamu adalah cinta terakhir aku, the person I truly wanted to stay with until the end. But life is cruel sometimes. The same person who once healed me slowly became the reason why my heart now feels impossible to fix. And now, you are the deepest wound I have ever carried. What hurts the most is not the fact that you left. It is the fact that after all the tears, sleepless nights, and silent breakdowns, I still cannot hate you completely. You destroyed me so easily, yet my heart still searches for you in every memory that refuses to die. Sometimes I wish we had never met. Because losing you did not only take away someone I loved, it also killed the version of me that once believed in forever. And maybe the saddest part is knowing that while I am still drowning in the pain you left behind, you are probably already happy somewhere else, living peacefully as if I never meant anything to you at all. You once made me feel like the luckiest person alive, but in the end, you became the reason why I learned that love can slowly destroy someone without killing them completely. Because the worst feeling is watching the person you would die for slowly become someone who would not even care if you were the one dying inside. And honestly, a part of me will always bleed because of you. Because before becoming my deepest pain, you were once my greatest happiness.
